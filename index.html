Our Mission and Story

Sam taught 7th grade English and knew there had to be a better way to find literature that was relevant, meaningful, and accessible to all of her students. She set out to do something about it and attended Startup Weekend EdTech Denver where she met Christine, Eric, Evan, and Kay. Brought together by shared passions for literature and equity in education, this team of technologists and education professionals built Novela to support teachers in making sure all students get to see themselves in the stories read and studied in school.

Our Team

Our team, founded by a teacher, was formed at Techstars Startup Weekend Education - Denver to create a solution for finding culturally responsive literature. We are made up of educators, marketers, designers, and developers who are passionate about equity in education. Our aim is to support teachers in ensuring every student gets to see themselves in the stories told and studied in school.
